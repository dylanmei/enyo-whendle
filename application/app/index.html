Whendle is an alarm-clock application. Whendle manages multiple alarms and includes an exhibition mode to display the current time.

This is an early release version of the TouchPad edition of Whendle. Some edges are still rough, and many of the features available in the original Whendle are still on their way!

The default alarm music is Audrey by @johnny_ripper (CC BY-SA 3.0) from the album soundtrack for a film that doesn't exist.

Whendle is availble under the terms of the MIT license, and the source is available on Github. If you have questions or feedback, visit whendle.com or send an email to whendle.app@gmail.com.
