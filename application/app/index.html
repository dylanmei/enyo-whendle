Whendle is a world timekeeping application. Whendle organizes clocks and alarms for your favorite places in the world.

This is a beta version of the TouchPad edition. Many of the features available in the original version are still on their way!

The default alarm music is Audrey by @johnny_ripper (CC BY-SA 3.0) from the album soundtrack for a film that doesn't exist.

Whendle is availble under the terms of the MIT license. The source is available on Github! If you have questions or feedback, visit whendle.com or send an email to whendle.app@gmail.com.
